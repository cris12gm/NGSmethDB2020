NGSmethDB20 is a database and web-server to explore data of DNA methylation from bisulfite sequencing experiments (WGBS and RRBS). It contains methylomes from 5 different species: Homo sapiens (hg38), Mus musculus (mm10), Pan troglodytes (pantro4), Macaca mulatta (rhemac3), Arabidopsis thaliana (tair10) and Solanum lycopersicum (sl2.50).
